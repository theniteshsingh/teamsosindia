About TEAM SOS INDIA

Team SOS India is a volunteer driven initiative which formally came into existence on 21st June 2020 with the objective to build India’s biggest network so that help can reach the needy at the time of distress at the earliest. Our aim is confined to not just helping people in distress but also to help people who want to take up some social initiative but are unable due to lack of support or resources. Team SOS India has more than 10k volunteers along with local social activists as partners all across the country covering all 743 districts in India.

Vision

Using the technology, innovation and power of social media along with the network of on-ground volunteers to cover whole India and work for the betterment of the humankind.

Mission

Our current mission is as per our present campaign – Rakhtsatyagrah . #Mission2024: which means that by the year 2024 no person will die due to blood shortage.

Aim

To build India’s largest network so that help can reach in every corner of India in no time.
