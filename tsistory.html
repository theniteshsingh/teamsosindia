Nitesh Singh is a Social Activist, avid reader and a polyglot (Marathi,Punjabi, bhojpuri, Hindi, English). Nitesh Singh, who founded Team SOS India in 21 June 2020, is currently working on Rakta Satyagrah #MissionMillion - to have 10,00,000 members of Team SOS India Army by Year 2024. TSI Army will work as one of the first responders to any calamity(Apart from medical aid, Team SOS India also works for Education for All, Rape Free India.).

Between October- November, Nitesh will visit every district in India as per part of Rakta Satyagrah(#Mission2024 #MissionMillion). For Rakta Satyagrah, he is currently taking language training in Tulu, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam and Tamil.

Nitesh's work featured on CNN International, DW news, DW News and Daily Pioneer
